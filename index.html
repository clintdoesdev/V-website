FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you join.

What is the Vireon website?
The Vireon website (vireonwebsite.com.ng) is the official home of Vireon — an international platform where you register once for Vireon Premiere and unlock every earning feature: Survey, Converse, CallCash, IQ, and more, plus financial tools like QuickLoan and EasyOwn. Read our platform safety guide if you're unsure you're on the official site.
Does it cost anything to join Vireon?
Yes. There's one package — Vireon Premiere — priced at a one-time ₦14,500. That single registration unlocks every earning feature on the platform, with no separate tiers or recurring charges. See the Payments page for the full checkout breakdown.
What do I get with Vireon Premiere?
Vireon Premiere is a one-time ₦14,500 registration that unlocks unlimited daily task submissions, 24-hour priority withdrawals, and bonus multipliers across CallCash and Streak Reward. See the Vireon Premiere page for the full benefits list, or the registration guide to get started.
How do I get paid?
Every approved task, review, call, or activity adds to your Vireon balance in real time. Once you reach the withdrawal threshold for a feature, you can request a payout from your dashboard.
Can I use more than one earning feature at once?
Yes. Most users combine several features — for example, Surveys and Streak Reward together — to maximise their weekly earnings from a single account.
How does CallCash verify a qualifying call?
CallCash tracks call duration through the connected feature in-app, and rewards are calculated automatically once a call meets the minimum qualifying length.
Is my data and payment information safe?
Vireon never shares your personal data with third parties without consent, and all payment information is handled through secure, encrypted channels.
Which countries can join Vireon?
Vireon is built as an international platform. Some features, like UK-brand surveys, are tailored to specific regions, while others such as Converse and Streak Reward are open globally.
