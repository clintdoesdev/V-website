# What is Vireon?

Vireon is an international digital earning platform. It connects users with paid opportunities —
surveys, remote client work, social monetization, and voice-call rewards — plus two financial
tools (a short-term loan and a buy-now-pay-later service). Everything runs through a single paid
membership called **Vireon Premiere**.

- **Official website:** [vireonwebsite.com.ng](https://vireonwebsite.com.ng/)
- **Support email:** support@vireonwebsite.com.ng
- **Official Telegram (activation & payment proof):** https://t.me/m/VGV7OU3LM2Zk
- **Target market:** Nigeria (site is in English, priced in Naira, geo-targeted `en-NG`)

> Only ever register, pay, or share details through the official domain above. Vireon staff never
> ask for your password, OTP, or card PIN, and never sell "free" registration through third-party
> links or unofficial groups (see `vireon-platform.html` for the full safety guide).

## How registration works

1. **Visit the official site** and confirm the address bar reads `vireonwebsite.com.ng`.
2. **Fill in the registration form** (`register.html`) — full name, email, phone number, password.
3. **Pay the one-time Vireon Premiere fee** via the Paystack checkout (₦15,000 total, including
   the ₦500 processing charge).
4. **Send payment proof to the official Telegram** — the registration form itself redirects
   straight to Telegram (https://t.me/m/VGV7OU3LM2Zk) after submission, and `payments.html` also
   links there for anyone who paid without registering first.
5. **Get activated** and start using every earning feature immediately — there is no separate
   activation step or waiting tier.

A shorter path exists too: `signup.html` is a two-field quick-start form (name + phone) that feeds
straight into the full registration form on `register.html`.

- **Vireon QuickLoan** — short-term loans without collateral, for emergencies, business needs,
  school fees, or personal expenses. Interest from as low as 4%/month, fast approval.
- **Vireon EasyOwn** — buy-now-pay-later for phones, gadgets, groceries, fashion, and household
  essentials, with the balance repaid automatically from Vireon earnings.

## How it works, end to end

1. **Create your account** — register and pay the one-time Vireon Premiere fee.
2. **Choose your path** — surveys, remote work, social monetization, CallCash, or the financial
   tools, or any combination.
3. **Complete & submit** — every task, call, or review is verified for quality before it counts.
4. **Withdraw earnings** — once a feature's payout threshold is reached, request a withdrawal from
   the dashboard; Premiere members get 24-hour priority processing.
